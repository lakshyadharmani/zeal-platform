Investor Thesis

Why Invest in
ZEAL?

The UAE has 700,000+ university students and no dedicated talent platform. The market is ready.

$4.2B

Total Addressable Market

UAE + GCC edtech and talent-tech market projected to reach $4.2B by 2028, growing at 18% CAGR. ZEAL targets the intersection of three underpenetrated verticals: edtech, HR tech, and competition platforms.

700K+

Immediate User Base

UAE alone has 700,000+ university students with no dedicated platform serving their career and competition needs. Expanding to GCC unlocks a 3.2M student market in Phase 2 within 18 months.

4×

Revenue Per User vs India

UAE's higher GDP per capita means students pay 4x more for premium features vs Indian platforms like Unstop. Companies spend 10x more per hire in the UAE. Unit economics are fundamentally superior.

2031

UAE Vision Tailwinds

UAE Vision 2031, Operation 300bn, and ADIO initiatives mandate Emiratization of private sector jobs and upskilling of youth. ZEAL is a direct enabler of national policy, making government partnerships natural.
